Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, butter, tomatoes, cream, spices.

Instructions: Cook chicken with spices. Prepare tomato-based sauce with butter. Mix and serve.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients: Paneer, yogurt, spices, bell peppers.

Instructions: Marinate paneer, grill it, and serve hot.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Rice batter, potatoes, curry leaves.

Instructions: Make dosa, fill with spiced potato mix, and serve with chutney.

Chole Bhature

Ingredients: Chickpeas, flour, yogurt, spices.

Instructions: Cook chickpeas with spices. Fry bhature and serve together.

Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, chicken/mutton, spices, yogurt.

Instructions: Marinate meat, cook rice separately, then layer and cook on low heat.

Rasgulla

Ingredients: Paneer, sugar, water, cardamom.

Instructions: Boil paneer dumplings in sugar syrup and serve chilled.
